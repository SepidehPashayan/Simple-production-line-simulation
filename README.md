In this project , the production line is simulated in such a way that Machine A and Machine B work simultaneously.
Machine A produces Piece 1 in 3 seconds with a 1% chance of failure.
Machine B produces Piece 2 in 4 seconds with a 3% chance of failure.
If both Piece 1 and Piece 2 are successfully produced , Machine C produces Piece 3 in 2 seconds with a 5% chance of failure.
And if Piece 3 is produced , Machine D produces Piece 4 in 2 seconds with a 2% chance of failure.
And the production is complete.
